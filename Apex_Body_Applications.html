Curricular & Co-Curricular Activities

Curricular Activities:

• Teaching by efficient experienced staff. Teachers are appointed only after evaluation of teaching demonstration.
• College is running only D.Pharma. As such, personal attention on one to one basis is given to each student with regard to learning and general behavior.
• During each academic year three sessional examinations are held covering 1/3 syllabus for each examination.
• Tests after completing each chapter are done and evaluation by concerned teacher to ensure understanding of the subject by the student.
• Preparatory examination is conducted before each annual examination o test the readiness of the students to face the examination successfully.

Co-curricular Activities:

• Equal importance is given to extra-curricular activities for overall development of the students. Annual sports and cultural competition are held every year.


Scholarships :

• Economically weaker students will be offered scholarships. Deserving bright students / Rank students are also offered fee concessional facility.

Achievements:
